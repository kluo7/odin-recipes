Korean Bulgogi Bowl

What is so good about the Korean Bulgogi Bowl?

The Korean Bulgogi Bowl is the staple of what defines Korean Food. This is an amazing twist with serving Bulgogi Beef. Bulgogi beef is served are thinly shaved slices of beef that are marinated and flame licked. This declious taste of ground beef in combination with rice will blow you away!

Ingredients

  • Lean Ground Beef
  • Sesame Oil
  • Brown Sugar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Water
  • Grated Asian Pear
  • Minced Garlic
  • Cucumber, red bell pepper, lettuce
  • Black pepper, red pper flakes, ginger
  • Cooked Brown Rice, sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in skillet unitl browned, about 5-7 minutes. Mix in sesame oil when done.
  2. Combine brown sugar, soy sauce, water, pear, garlic, ginger, red pepper flakes, black pepper in blender. Blend together and then pour mixture into skillet with beef.
  3. Place cooked brown rice in the bottome of serving bowl. Top it off with beef, lettuce, cucumber, red bell pepper, seasme seeds.
  4. Enjoy your meal!
