Zucchini Pasta

Why is Zucchini Pasta so Good?

This is a delicious bowl of pasta served with zucchini in a garlicky sauce. It is the perfect dish for a late night after work. You can complement this dish with crusty bread and a chilled white wine if desired.

Ingredients

  • Rotini Pasta
  • Small sliced zucchini
  • Olive Oil
  • Minced Garlic
  • Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
  • Chopped Fresh Parsley
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Use a large pot to boil pasta in salted water. Cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente.
  2. Fill a medium sauce pan with lightly salted water. Add zucchini and bring to a boil for 10 minutes.
  3. In a large skillet, saute garlic oil and hot pepper flakes. Add drained zucchini and parsley, then mix all together and simmer for 5 to 10 minutes.
