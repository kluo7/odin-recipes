Scott Hibb's Amazing Whisky Grilled Baby Back Ribs

Why are Scott Hibb's Whisky Grilled Baby Back Ribs so Amazing?

Scott Hibb's famous Whisky Grilled Baby Back Ribs are the perfect dish to go along with the event! The meat on the bone is so tender and juicy where you cant resist taking another bite.

Ingredients

  • Baby Back Pork Ribs
  • Black Pepper
  • Red Chile Pepper
  • Vegatable Oil
  • Minced Onion
  • Water
  • Tomato Paste
  • Any other Spieces you would like to add!

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees
  2. Cut the rack of ribs into halfs
  3. Sprinkle salt and pepper onto each rack of rib. Wrap each half in aluminum foil and back for 2 1/2 hours.
  4. Heat oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat and cook the onions for 5 minutes
  5. Stir in water, tomato paste, and other seasonings that you would like to add to your paste
  6. Preheat an outdoor grill on high heat
  7. Remove Ribs from oven and let it sit for 10 minutes
  8. Remove Ribs from foil and grill ribs for 3 to 4 minutes to each side. While grilling, brush sauce on each side of the rib to maintain consistency.
  9. After grilling ribs for 3-4 minutes on each side, brush sauce on it after removal
  10. Enjoy your Ribs!
